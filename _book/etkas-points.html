4.1 ETKAS points

When entering in waiting list candidates’ Mismatch Probability is computed as the probability of receiving a kidney offer with 0 or 1 HLA-A, -B, -DR mismatch based on 1000 kidneys offered. This MMP can be calculated based on Dutch or Portuguese HLA antigen frequencies (this is a KARS app option). Detailed formulas can be found here.

Final points for each eligible candidate are obtain from the sum of HLA points, dialysis points (as described in table 4.1) and MMP points.

In the simulations performed within KARS app, no regional or country points are considered!
